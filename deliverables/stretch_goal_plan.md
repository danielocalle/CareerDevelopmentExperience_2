# Stretch Goal Plan

## Context

- Role: TA, CPTS 321
- Time window: `01/12/2026` to `04/20/2026` (evidence snapshot used in this report)
- CYA category: stretch goal or measurable impact in current role

## Truth-First Goal

Document clear TA actions that required extra effort and had visible impact, without claiming metrics I did not track.

## Stretch Actions

1. Return grades and exams quickly when possible, including assignments turned around in under 24 hours.
2. Offer flexible follow-up conversations for students requesting clarification or point recovery.
3. Provide actionable guidance during follow-ups instead of only yes/no point decisions.

## What I Can Measure Honestly

1. Number of assignments with <24-hour grading turnaround.
2. Number of exams with a following Monday grading turnaround.
3. Estimated number of student follow-up or point-recovery discussions handled.
4. Estimated additional time spent on follow-ups.

## Estimation Rules

1. Mark estimates clearly with `~` or `>`.
2. Explain estimate logic directly in the measurable impact report.
3. Separate directly visible evidence (screenshots) from privacy-limited evidence (student correspondence).
