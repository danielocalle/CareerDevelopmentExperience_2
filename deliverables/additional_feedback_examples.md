# Additional Feedback And Flexibility Examples

This file documents examples of extra support provided during the CYA stretch effort.

## Example 1: Fast Turnaround

- Assignment: `HW0`
- Action: Returned grades in under 24 hours.
- Evidence: `artifacts/HW0-gradingunder24hrs.png`
- Why this matters: Since this assignment was setting up GitLab repositories, it was vital for students to get feedback as soon as possible, since every future assignment was built in these repositories. 

## Example 2: Point-Recovery Discussion

- Assignment: `HW5`
- Student request: Asked to review rubric interpretation and potential points back.
- Action taken:
  - Re-read submission and rubric criteria.
  - Researched appropriate design principles, alternative implementations, and justification for the original deduction.
  - Responded with detailed clarification.
  - When met with further interpretation and clarification requests, reached out to the Professor personally to get quick feedback for the student.
  - Restored points when it was discovered that the rubric was not totally aligned with what was taught in class.
- Evidence: Internal private correspondence and rubric review notes (not published for student privacy).
- Impact: Improved fairness and student trust, while increasing TA time investment. This also helped other students who may have been impacted by a similar deduction.

## Example 3: Exam Weekend Grading Turnaround

- Assessment: `MT1` (midterm 1)
- Action taken:
  - Met with fellow TA and professor on exam day to align grading decisions.
  - Spent Friday through Sunday finishing exam grading.
  - Completed return coordination by Monday (`02/23/2026`).
- Evidence: `artifacts/MT1-gradingbyfollowingmonday.png`
- Impact: Students got their exam grades as soon as possible, allowing them to re-evaluate their course performance sooner rather than later.
