# Measurable Impact Report

## Scope

This report summarizes impact from my TA stretch effort in CPTS 321.

- Measurement period (evidence snapshot): `01/12/2026` to `04/20/2026`
- Total additional time invested (estimated): `~11` hours
- Privacy constraint: student emails/messages are not published; related metrics are explicitly marked as estimates

## Metric Definitions And Estimation Method

1. `<24-hour homework turnaround` is counted when a dated TA update shows grading completion on the same day.
2. `MT1 Monday turnaround` is counted from dated evidence showing Monday completion/return coordination after a Friday exam cycle.
3. `Follow-up/point-recovery discussions (>15)` is estimated from private correspondence volume across the measurement window.
4. `Additional follow-up hours (~7)` is estimated from about 30-60 minutes per week over ~10 active weeks.
5. `Total additional effort (~11)` combines follow-up time (~7 hours) plus extra non-routine grading/coordination effort (~4 hours).
